Our Story

Fourteen Years of Excellence.

Kiya & Associates Law Office is a law firm based in Addis Ababa, that represents the business interests of foreign multinational companies. With expertise in business and investment law, mergers and acquisitions, intellectual property law, and labor and employment law, we provide comprehensive legal services to clients across various industries.

Kiya & Associates Law Office was established and currently managed by Kiya Tsegaye, a seasoned lawyer, adept negotiator, and prominent public figure. The firm employs a team of well-versed attorneys who are adept at navigating the complex regulatory and bureaucratic landscape of Ethiopia. We assist our clients in matters of obtaining licenses, government relations, ensuring legal compliance, managing new legislation that impact their business operations, addressing tax matters, and preventing litigation.

Frequently Asked Questions

We primarily represent the business interests of foreign multinational companies operating or looking to invest in Ethiopia. We serve clients across various industries, including manufacturing, technology, agriculture, and services.

Our core areas of practice include business and investment law, mergers and acquisitions (M&A), intellectual property law, and labor and employment law. We also provide comprehensive support in regulatory compliance, tax matters, and government relations.

Our team is committed to continuous professional development. We actively monitor legislative changes, participate in legal forums, and engage with regulatory bodies to stay at the forefront of legal developments that may impact our clients' businesses.

The process typically begins with an initial consultation to understand your specific needs and objectives. Following this, we will provide a detailed proposal outlining the scope of work, our legal strategy, and the associated fee structure. Once we have an agreement, our team will commence work on your case.

Client confidentiality is of utmost importance to us. We adhere to strict confidentiality protocols and professional ethics to ensure that all client information is securely handled and protected. Our engagement agreements explicitly outline our commitment to confidentiality.
