Who We Are

Kiya & Associates Law Office.

Headquartered in Addis Ababa, Kiya & Associates stands as a pioneering force in shaping the future of business and investment law in Ethiopia and beyond. Under the visionary leadership of Kiya Tsegaye—an accomplished lawyer and master negotiator—the firm empowers multinational companies to navigate opportunities with confidence.

  • Unmatched expertise in mergers & acquisitions, intellectual property, labor law, and investment advisory.
  • Proven track record in successfully navigating complex legal landscapes.
  • Blending global standards with local insight.
  • Seamless solutions in licensing, government relations, compliance, and tax.
  • Renowned for agility, integrity, and excellence

With a strong network and deep understanding of Ethiopia’s regulatory framework, Kiya & Associates are more than legal advisors—they are trusted partners committed to driving their clients’ success on the international stage.
